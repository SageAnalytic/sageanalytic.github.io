Service Notice

The Sage Analytic data center suffered a hardware failure on October 24, 2018 and all of our Internet services are now offline.

Unfortunately, this event will spell the permanent demise of our magicVORTEX File Transfer Service. We had hoped to keep this service running until a better, more-reliable home could be found, but fate had its way. magicVORTEX had pioneered the peer-to-peer transfer concept back in 2000. So after 18 years, we just couldn't afford to keep the service available for our loyal users once the hardware broke down.

For alternatives, you might consider:

    Dropbox
    Box
    Egnyte
